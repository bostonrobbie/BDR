2

3
Proof: "One [vertical] team we work with..." + specific stat. No named customers. +9.0pp "test maintenance" / +7.4pp "release cycles"
4
Why you (TIIWW): "Thought it would be worth reaching out because [specific trigger — job posting, company signal, role change, tech stack]." +12.6pp
5
Open question: "Is that something you're seeing?" / "Does that resonate?" / "Is that relevant to what you're working on?" — NO meeting ask, NO "What day works"
6
Sig + intro (BOTTOM): Rob Gorham | Testsigma + one line: "We have yet to be properly introduced. I work with [QA/Eng] leaders at enterprise companies."
