Copy-Paste Workflow & Pacing Rules

Step 1: Open Tracker HTML

Open the Batch 7 or Batch 8 tracker (check Batch column in queue below) in a new tab. Keep it open for copy-paste.

Step 2: Find Prospect in Tracker

Ctrl+F the prospect's name in the tracker HTML. Locate their message block.

Step 3: Copy Subject Line

Click "Copy Subject" button. This copies the subject line to your clipboard.

Step 4: Copy Message Body

Click "Copy Message" button. This copies the full InMail message body.

Step 5: Open LinkedIn Sales Nav

Click the LinkedIn URL in the queue table (or paste it). Open the prospect's Sales Navigator profile.

Step 6: Send InMail

Click "Message" on their profile. Paste subject (if field exists). Paste body. Submit to Claude for approval. Rob approves and clicks Send.
