APPROVED FIXES BY CATEGORY
───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

FIX #1: MULTIPLIER FRAMING
  Find:       "5X faster", "4X faster", "3X faster", etc.
  Replace:    "cut by 80%", "reduced from X to Y weeks", "cut execution by NN%"
  Examples:   "5X faster execution" → "cut execution time by 80%"
              "4X faster test execution" → "reduced from 4 weeks to 1 week"

FIX #2: HYPHENS
  Keep Only:  self-healing, auto-healing, cross-browser, e-prescribing, zero-defect
  Remove:     banking-as-service, pixel-perfect, micro-investing, compliance-grade,
              ai-driven, e-commerce (unless e-prescribing), low-code, multi-market,
              fda-regulated, patient-facing, regression-critical, large-scale,
              audit-critical, anti-money

FIX #3: PROOF POINTS
  Insert:     Use one of these approved customer stories:
              • "Hansard cut regression from 8 weeks to 5"
              • "CRED automated 90% of regression"
              • "Medibuddy built 2,500 tests and cut maintenance by 50%"
              • "Sanofi cut regression from 3 days to 80 minutes"
              • "Freshworks reduced test flakiness at scale"
              • "Spendflo cut manual testing by 50%"
              • "Nagra DTV built 2,500 tests in 8 months, 4X faster"

FIX #4: BUZZWORDS
  Replace:    "bandwidth" → "team capacity" or "testing resources"
              "velocity" → "speed" or "execution time"
              "bottleneck" → "constraint" or "limiting factor"

FIX #5: HC2 OPENS (Optional)
  Current:    "Self-healing is cutting edge..."
  Better:     "As your platform grows, test maintenance becomes harder.
               Hansard cut regression from 8→5 weeks..."
