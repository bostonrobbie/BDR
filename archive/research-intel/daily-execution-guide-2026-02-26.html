Hard weekly ceiling across all types. Plan 4 send days/week to stay at ~5/day average.

Pacing Rule: Space sends 2-3 minutes apart. Take a 10-minute break after every 5 sends. Never send back-to-back days at identical times.

Daily Send Routine (Copy This Checklist)

Pre-Session (5 min) — 11:50 AM

  • Open Sales Navigator in Chrome
  • Confirm LinkedIn session is active (not logged out)
  • Check InMail credits remaining (note count)
  • Open today's batch tracker HTML file
  • Identify today's 6-8 prospects from the schedule

Send Loop (45-60 min) — 12:00 PM

Repeat for each prospect:

  • Step 1: Navigate to prospect's Sales Navigator profile URL
  • Step 2: Pre-flight check — Name matches? Title matches? No "Messaged:" indicator?
  • Step 3: Click "Message" button to open InMail composer
  • Step 4: Copy subject line from tracker → Paste into Subject field
  • Step 5: Copy message body from tracker → Paste into message field
  • Step 6: Verify paragraph breaks survived the paste (fix if collapsed)
  • Step 7: Final eye check — no placeholders, name spelled right, looks clean
  • Step 8: Click Send
  • Step 9: Update status in tracker to "Touch 1 Sent" (or Touch 2/3)
  • Step 10: Wait 2-3 minutes before next prospect

Post-Session (5 min)

  • Verify all statuses updated in tracker
  • Note InMail credits remaining (should match: start - sends)
  • Check for any immediate replies
  • Save tracker file
  • Plan tomorrow's send list

Quick Reference: Copy-Paste Workflow

For Each Prospect Send:

  1. Open the tracker HTML (5A or 5B)
  2. Find the prospect card (sorted by priority, highest first)
  3. Click the touch tab (Touch 1, Touch 2, or Touch 3)
  4. Click "Copy Subject" button → paste into InMail/Email subject
  5. Click "Copy Message" button → paste into InMail/Email body
  6. Quick visual check: Name right? Paragraph breaks intact? Clean on screen?
  7. Send
  8. Update status dropdown in the tracker
