## FAQ

**Q: Should I enroll before or after Touch 1 sends?**
A: After Touch 1 sends are complete (Feb 27-28). This ensures we have the actual send timestamps in Apollo.

**Q: What if a prospect already exists in Apollo from another BDR?**
A: Check ownership. If owned by another BDR, skip enrollment (don't duplicate). If owned by Rob or >6 months old, re-enroll.

**Q: Should I manually log Touch 1/2 InMails to Apollo, or does it auto-log?**
A: InMails sent via LinkedIn do NOT auto-log to Apollo (LinkedIn integration is limited). Manually update Apollo contact notes with send dates, or rely on Gmail Touch 3 for auto-logging.

**Q: Can I batch-enroll all 25 at once?**
A: Yes, but phased enrollment (P5 → P4 → P3) is safer. Allows early replies to be tracked, and avoids mass-enrolling unresponded prospects.

**Q: What if an InMail bounces or fails to send?**
A: Mark status in batch tracker as "Bounced" or "Error". Skip enrollment for that prospect (they can't receive emails). Note the reason for removal.
