6
Warm Lead Response
PRIORITY
▼
Purpose: Respond to positive replies same-day. Do this whenever a reply comes in, not just at end of day.
  • Read the reply carefully — what triggered it? (Tag: Opener, Pain hook, Proof point, Timing, Referral, Not interested)
  • Update reply tag in tracker
  • IF POSITIVE ("interested," "tell me more," "good timing"): Mirror language, 1 proof point, bridge to meeting ("What day works this week for a quick look?")
  • Create Google Calendar follow-up reminder (2 business days out)
  • IF REFERRAL ("talk to [name]"): Thank referrer (1-2 lines), research referred person (3 sources)
  • Draft outreach to referred person mentioning referrer by name
  • IF NEGATIVE ("not interested"): Log reason, update status to "Not Interested"
  • IF MEETING BOOKED: Update status, generate prep card, create calendar event, celebrate!
⚡ CRITICAL: Warm leads get response priority. Check email and LinkedIn multiple times throughout the day, not just at scheduled send times.
