## Prospect Persona Deep-Dive

### Primary Decision-Maker Personas (Ranked by Likelihood to Reply)

#### 1. **VP Engineering / CTO** (High Pain, Lower Reply Rate: ~0.6-0.8%)
- **Most Responsive At:** Smaller companies (50-200 ppl) or companies in transformation mode
- **Pain Trigger:** Release velocity blocked by testing
- **Best Message Angle:** "Testing is slowing your innovation cycle"
- **Best Proof Point:** Speed/velocity stories (CRED 5x faster, Hansard 8→5 weeks)
- **Example Companies:** loanDepot (new CDO/CIO), Aventiv ($360M invest), TriNetX, NCR Voyix

#### 2. **Director of QA / Head of QA** (Direct Pain, Higher Reply Rate: ~1.0-1.4%)
- **Most Responsive At:** Any company size
- **Pain Trigger:** Manual testing backlog, flaky tests, compliance burden
- **Best Message Angle:** "Your regression test backlog is growing faster than your team"
- **Best Proof Point:** Maintenance reduction (70%, 90%), coverage stories (CRED 90% coverage)
- **Example Companies:** All 8 (primary persona across all)

#### 3. **VP QA / Quality Engineering** (Direct Pain, Highest Reply Rate: ~1.2-1.6%)
- **Most Responsive At:** Larger companies (500+ ppl) or highly regulated (finance, healthcare, corrections)
- **Pain Trigger:** Test execution velocity, compliance testing burden
- **Best Message Angle:** "Compliance + complexity = your team is maxed out"
- **Best Proof Point:** Sanofi (pharma, 3 days→80 min), Hansard (insurance, 8→5 weeks)
- **Example Companies:** TriNetX, loanDepot, Aventiv, NCR Voyix

#### 4. **Senior SDET / QA Automation Lead** (Technical Influencer, Variable Reply Rate: ~0.8-1.2%)
- **Most Responsive At:** Companies with modern test frameworks or active tech debt payoff
- **Pain Trigger:** Brittleness in tests, AI-generated tests breaking, cross-platform parity
- **Best Message Angle:** "Self-healing would eliminate your test maintenance backlog"
- **Best Proof Point:** Maintenance reduction (70%), auto-healing capability
- **Example Companies:** 2K Games, NCR Voyix, loanDepot (microservices)
