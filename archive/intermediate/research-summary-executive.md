## Questions & Clarifications

**Q: Should I reach out to multiple people at the same company?**
A: Yes, but space them out by 5-7 days. Start with VP Eng/Director of QA, then follow up with SDET/QA Lead if no reply.

**Q: Which proof point should I use first?**
A: Use Hansard or CRED first (speed/velocity angle). If no reply, switch to Medibuddy (scale/maintenance). Track what works.

**Q: Should I mention the specific trigger event (e.g., "Saw you announced Securus One")?**
A: Yes, but subtly. Don't cite the event; synthesize it into a sharp insight. Example: "Platform consolidation usually means testing velocity becomes the bottleneck."

**Q: How long should I wait before moving a prospect to "Not Interested"?**
A: Full 6-touch sequence (Day 1, 3, 5, 8, 10, 15) = ~2.5 weeks. If no reply after Touch 6, move to Dormant.
